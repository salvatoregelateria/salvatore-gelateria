Our Story

We’re a small, family-owned gelateria bringing the flavors of Italy to our Elk Grove community. Inspired by traditional Italian gelaterias, we handcraft every batch of gelato in-house using time-honored recipes and real ingredients, many straight from Italy. From classics like pistachio and stracciatella to seasonal and dairy-free favorites, there’s something here for everyone. For us, it’s more than just dessert, it’s about sharing love, laughter, and a little scoop of joy. Thanks for being part of our story.

Traditional Recipes

Each flavor is crafted using authentic Italian techniques and recipes passed down through our family for generations.

Fresh Daily

We make our gelato fresh every day using only the finest imported and locally sourced ingredients.

Family Owned

As a family-owned business, we take pride in serving our community of Elk Grove with warmth and authentic Italian hospitality.
